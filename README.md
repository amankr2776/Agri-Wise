## 🌱 What is AgriWise?

**AgriWise** is an AI-powered companion built for the Indian farmer — the kind who may not read fluently, may not trust an app screen over decades of field experience, and may not know if today's mandi price is fair. AgriWise meets that farmer where they are: **speak instead of type, listen instead of read, snap a photo instead of describing a symptom.**

It combines a **40+ crop diagnostic database**, **live market intelligence**, **logistics booking**, and a **farmer social network** — wrapped in a voice-first, multilingual interface that works for the literate and illiterate alike.

> *"Kheti ki samajh, ab har bhasha mein."*
> — *Farming wisdom, now in every language.*

## ✨ Core Features

### 🩺 AI-Driven Crop & Pest Diagnostics
The intelligence engine at AgriWise's heart.

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| 📸 **Image & Symptom Analysis** | Upload a photo of an affected crop/pest, or describe symptoms in your own language — AI reasons through both. |
| 🧪 **Chemical Cures** | Scientific, dosage-specific pesticide/fungicide guidance (e.g., *Flubendiamide* for Paddy, *Propiconazole* for Wheat). |
| 🌿 **Desi Nuskha (Traditional Wisdom)** | Organic, low-cost remedies — sour buttermilk, cow urine, neem cake — passed down through generations. |
| ✅ **Expert Verification** | Agricultural experts (e.g., `expert_rahul`) review and **Certify** entries, separating verified science from rumor. |
| 🗳️ **Nuskha Voting** | The farming community upvotes/downvotes traditional remedies based on real field results. |

### 📈 Live Market Intelligence
Solving *information asymmetry* — so no farmer sells short.

- **Real-time Price Tracking** across states (Punjab, Haryana, Karnataka & more)
- **AI Trend Analysis** — instantly tags prices as `📈 Rising`, `➖ Stable`, or `📉 Falling`
- **Smart Price Alerts** — auto-flags to government authorities when prices spike **20%+** above average, guarding against manipulation

### 🚚 Integrated Logistics — *Mandi-Link*
Bridging farm gate to market gate.

- **Fleet Directory** — from 🛺 E-Rickshaw Loaders to 🧊 Refrigerated Vans
- **Transparent Pricing** — price-per-km and load capacity (1 Ton – 20 Tons) shown upfront, before booking

### 🤝 Kisan Network — Community & Social
Peer wisdom, crowdsourced and mapped.

- 📰 **Agricultural Social Feed** — post updates, share crop photos, like helpful posts
- 🐛 **Pest Alert Crowdsourcing** — report sightings with GPS lat/long → live outbreak heatmap for the whole community
- 🗣️ **Nuskha Voting** — community-validated traditional remedies

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Multi-Stakeholder Dashboards

| Role | Dashboard Highlights |
|---|---|
| 👨‍🌾 **Farmer** | AI diagnostics, market prices, logistics booking, social feed |
| 🧑‍🔬 **Expert / NGO** | Certify remedies, provide field support, review crowdsourced reports |
| 🏛️ **Government / Authority** | High-level stats, pest outbreak maps, price-manipulation alerts |
| 🚛 **Logistics Provider** | Manage fleet, update contact details & pricing |

### 🎙️ "Talk to my Farm" — Voice & Language
Accessibility isn't an afterthought — it's the foundation.

- 🌐 **11 Languages**: Hindi, English, Bhojpuri, Bengali, Marathi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Punjabi, Haryanvi
- 🔊 **Voice-First UI** — every screen, result, and remedy can be read aloud via the Web Speech API
- 💬 **AI Voice Chat Assistant** — ask any farming question out loud, get a spoken answer back, no typing required
- 👁️ **Built for Illiteracy-Inclusive Access** — designed so a farmer who cannot read can still diagnose, sell, and connect

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

| Principle | Approach |
|---|---|
| **Layout** | Clean, intuitive, device-agnostic — clear hierarchy for diagnostics, market data & community feed |
| **Typography** | `PT Sans` — warm, legible, professional |
| **Iconography** | Illustrative, nature-rooted icons (crop, pest, mandi, community) |
| **Animation** | Subtle, functional micro-feedback on posting, querying, and filtering — never distracting |
| **Accessibility** | Voice-first, multilingual by default — not bolted on |
