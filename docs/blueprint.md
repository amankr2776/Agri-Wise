# **App Name**: AgriWise

## Core Features:

- AI-Driven Crop & Pest Diagnostics (Image & Symptom Analysis): Farmers can input crop type and symptoms or upload images of crops/pests to receive AI-driven chemical pesticide/fungicide and traditional 'Desi Nuskha' remedy suggestions as a diagnostic tool. The AI tool will use reasoning to suggest appropriate solutions based on the input.
- Expert Remedy Verification: Designated agricultural experts can review and 'Certify' diagnostic suggestions and traditional remedies, ensuring farmers receive verified, trustworthy information.
- Real-time Market Intelligence & Trends: Displays current market prices for crops across various states and employs AI to analyze and identify 'Rising,' 'Stable,' or 'Falling' price trends. Automatically flags 'Price Alerts' for government authorities when a crop price rises more than 20% above the average, helping to prevent market manipulation.
- Integrated Logistics Marketplace: A comprehensive directory allowing farmers to find specialized transport options with transparent pricing (price-per-km) and load capacities for efficient farm-to-market delivery.
- Kisan Social Network & Pest Alerting: A community feed where farmers can post updates, share photos, engage with posts, and report pest sightings with GPS coordinates to crowdsource real-time outbreak maps. Includes a 'Nuskha Voting' feature to upvote or downvote traditional remedies.
- Multi-Stakeholder Dashboards: Provides specialized, role-based dashboards for various user types including farmers, agricultural experts/NGOs (to verify data and provide field support), government authorities (with high-level stats, pest outbreak maps, and price alerts), and logistics providers (to manage fleets and pricing).
- Multilingual Voice Interface & Universal Audio Support: Offers support for a wide range of regional languages (e.g., Hindi, English, Bhojpuri, Bengali, Marathi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Punjabi, Haryanvi) for the app interface. It uses Web Speech API and other TTS tools to read all solutions and interface elements aloud at every interface, ensuring accessibility for illiterate farmers.
- Conversational AI Voice Assistant: Provides an AI-powered voice chat interface allowing farmers to ask questions in their preferred language and receive immediate, verbally delivered solutions and guidance on farming practices, market trends, or pest management. The AI tool will use reasoning to provide relevant answers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme. Primary color: a deep, earthy green (#3C862D), symbolizing growth, stability, and agricultural heritage. Background color: a subtle, very light greenish-grey (#F1F4F0) to ensure a clean, natural feel. Accent color: a vibrant yellow-green (#9DD926) for highlighting calls to action and adding a touch of vitality.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif. Its clean lines and warm character ensure high readability and a friendly, yet professional, appearance suitable for conveying crucial information to farmers.
- Utilize clear, illustrative icons with an agricultural or natural theme, simplifying navigation and visually reinforcing content related to crops, pests, markets, and community activities.
- Implement a clean, organized, and intuitive layout designed for ease of use across various device sizes. Prioritize clear information hierarchy for diagnostic results, market data, and community interactions.
- Incorporate subtle, functional animations for feedback on actions like posting updates, submitting diagnostic queries, or toggling data filters, enhancing the user experience without distraction.
